## Euler Foundaiton FAQs

### How does the Euler Foundation manage treasury funds?

The Euler DAO treasury is managed by the Euler Foundation through a combination of smart contracts and governance decisions, with strict protocols for fund allocation, investment, and disbursement. 

Transparency is maintained through regular audits and public reporting of treasury activities.
