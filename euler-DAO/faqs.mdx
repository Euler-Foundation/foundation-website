## Euler DAO FAQs

### What is the Euler DAO?

The Euler DAO is a decentralised autonomous organisation focused on advancing the interests of the Euler DeFi ecosystem and its stakeholders via governance mechanisms and community engagement. The Euler DAO is a group of people and builders across numerous countries, who may join voluntarily, if they wish to participate in the Euler ecosystem. More information may be found here.
