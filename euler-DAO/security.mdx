## Audits 

A total of 29 audit reports from 12 independent security firms were carried out ahead of the launch of Euler v2. 

For more information on Euler v2 audits, we recommend reading this blog post — [Security Euler](https://www.euler.finance/blog/securing-euler) — on the Euler Blog.
